Terms of Service

GENERAL

  • Must be 18+ or older

  • TOS will subject to change depending on commission volume

  • I won't redraw AI artwork nor to draw an artwork resembling another artist's artwork in style, composition, or specific elements.

  • Preferably you have non AI references since it is more clearer and believable in design, pose, and other details. If you have an OC in mind you want me to draw, give me the design sheet if available (AI Generated OC designs is a no go too, sorry).

  • I have my right to refuse orders that I am not comfortable completing.

PAYMENTS

  • You have to pay in full for me to start working on your commission.

  • All payments is done through VGen

REVISIONS

  • Rough sketch revisions up to 4 times, after that you have to choose between revisions that you 100% sure fits your needs.

  • Can only do small revisions during render phase.

  • Any changes that'll change the whole illustration during render cannot be done, thus you may want to ensure that the rough sketch layout is something you 100% approve of!

  • If you want to do another version you may want to do additional commission (s).

DEADLINES AND DELIVERY

  • When I start working on your commission, It'll be worked on around 2 days to 1-2 weeks (depending on the complexity of the commission)

  • Timeline

    • 1-3 days Sketch Phase

    • 2-3 days for Bust Up and Icons

    • 4 days to 2 weeks for Full Renders

USE

  • Personal use is free.

  • Commercial use is for every product purchased made with this artwork, this is intended if you're gonna use it to sell prints, shirts, and promotional materials.

  • Fee for commercial use is 100% the price of the service you asked me for.

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY RIGHTS

  • I hold all rights to the illustration. You may use it for reposting/sharing on social media. Please ask me personally for any questions.

  • If the request is a fanart, I may use it to sell prints for example.

  • You may repost/share the illustration on social media with proper credit. Any modifications to the artwork must be approved by me.

REFUNDS

  • You can only refund if I haven't started working on the illustration, which I will inform you first.

  • You cannot refund me during progress AND after completion, make sure you've 100% approve of the drafts I'll be communicating with you along the way.

COMMUNICATION

  • You can contact me through DM/Discord or Email. Expect a response within 24-48 hours range.

Future Plans

Will there be more types of commissions coming soon?

Yes, absolutely. But for now I want to test the waters, and am also working on getting used to working on comms and setting a good time schedule alongside comms and other projects.
