Featured

Commissions

Life

Today marks the day I’ve officially opened my commissions on VGen!

Time sure flies, I have to take multiple months to prep up payments (one hell of a detour for sure) and also praciting my skills to be good enough for what I intended for commissions including some artwork I’ll be using as samples.

It feels like a good breathe of fresh air that now I can open up and everything is set up.

As of this date, I’m open for 3 types of commissions:

  • Character Icons / Profile Picture
  • Thigh Up Character Illustrations
  • Full Body Character Illustrations
