Megumumpkin and The World of Xianxia

5.5.2023

Fantasy

Wuxia

Murim

Featured

Artwork

I like oriental power-leveling fiction, especially the ones deal with eastern magical powers like qi core building for example.

So in here we have Susie and “Me” showcasing our powers and readying ourselves for a journey full of tribulations in the murim world.

Susie is a humanoid doll controlled through spirit channeling, like multitasking by controlling both bodies at the same time which turns into a very close synchronous tag team action.

Murim in general is a realm different from mortal realm, which mortal realm is a place for humans with standard physical abilities, the contrary applies to murim which the humans in that realms practices strong powerful martial arts defying the laws of nature and they have their set of laws that applies differently from the mortal realm.

In the backdrop there’s a circular mural which is the symbol of ouroboros, which is the symbol of cycle of life and death. I do plan to use the buddhism’s samsara symbol, but when it is overlapped by the characters like the image above. It’ll break the symbol and would break the composition, so a more clear symbol like ouroboros is used instead.
